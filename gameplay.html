Gameplay

Creating a Separator in MO2

  1. Right-click the empty space in the left pane of MO2 and select Create separator.
  2. Name the separator Gameplay.

Survival Mode Improved - SKSE

  • Completely rewrites the needs systems in C++ for better performance, allowing them to update every few seconds.
  • Overhauls how needs work.
  • Removes the annoying start-up prompt.
  • Pauses needs progression during certain activities.

Installation instructions

  • Main Files - Survival Mode Improved - SKSE (1.6.1170)
  • A Dragonborn's Fate Resources
    • Main Files - Survival Mode Improved - SKSE - USSEP Patch

Encounter Zones Unlocked SE

Allows previously-visited encounter zones to update the levels of the enemies in them as the player levels up.

Installation instructions

  • Main Files - Encounter Zones Unlocked (1.6.X)

Enhanced Invisibility

Enhances invisibility effects with improved shaders, makes blood splatter and arrows invisible, and stops item interaction from dispelling invisibility.

Stamina of Shooting - drawing bow costs stamina

Keeping a bow drawn costs stamina. When you're out of stamina, the bow is let down.

Installation instructions

  • Main Files - Stamina of Shooting - drawing bow costs stamina
    • You will be prompted with a FOMOD installer: choose based on your preference (the default is fine), then Install.
